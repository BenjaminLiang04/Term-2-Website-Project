My Experience

My curiosity for Breath of the wild started when I first saw the trailer in 2017 and got interested. Later in the year when Breath of the Wild was officially out in stores, my sister and I went and bought it from the Nintendo store. Both of us had a lot of fun playing Breath of the Wild and I thought it would be nice if I could inform others about Breath of the Wild, therefore I chose Breath of the Wild as my topic.
