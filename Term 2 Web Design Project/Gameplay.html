Innovations

Breath of the Wild is a unique game to the Zelda franchise. Breath of the wild introduced features such as extremely realistic physics, a completely explorable 3d world, a vast variety of clothes and weapons, and weather conditions that had never been seen in a Zelda game before. Breath of the Wild has a charm that appeals to people of all ages. From its diverse amount of plant life to its mysterious plot, Breath of the Wild is truly one of the most unique games that Nintendo has ever made!
